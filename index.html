SPIKE PIT

PLAY

How to Play

Objective: Score points by making the ball fall through holes on your opponent's side, or by reducing their HP to zero.

Controls:

  • W/A/S/D - Move character
  • I - Serve/Spike/Receive (hold to charge serve)
  • I + W/S - Aim serve/spike direction
  • A + I - Spike serve (targets first lane)
  • P - Reset game

Gameplay:

  • Each character starts with 10 HP
  • Falling off the court reduces HP by 3
  • You have 5 touches per side before taking damage
  • Destroy tiles to create holes for scoring
  • First to 11 points or reduce opponent HP to 0 wins

Tips:

  • Use spike serves (A+I) to target specific lanes
  • Watch your touch count - exceeding it costs HP
  • Destroy tiles strategically to create scoring opportunities
  • Use the receive zone to automatically position for returns
