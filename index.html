SPIKE PIT

How to Play

Objective: Win by being the first to reach 11 points OR by reducing your opponent's HP to zero. Score points by making the ball fall through holes on your opponent's side.

Controls:

  • W/A/S/D - Move character
  • J - Jump
  • I - Serve/Spike/Receive (hold to charge serve)
  • O - Forward toss (works from spike zone, both ground and mid-air)

Most actions can be combined with directional keys (W/A/S/D) for aiming.

Gameplay:

  • Each character starts with 10 HP
  • Falling off the court reduces HP by 3
  • You have 5 touches per side before taking damage
  • Destroy tiles to create holes for scoring
  • First to 11 points or reduce opponent HP to 0 wins

Tips:

  • Hold I to charge your serve - release to serve
  • Press A + I while serving for a spike serve that targets specific lanes
  • Press O when the ball is in the spike zone to do a forward toss (slow, arcing trajectory)
  • The spike zone appears when you jump or when you're on the ground and the ball enters the zone
  • Watch your touch count - exceeding it costs HP
  • Destroy tiles strategically to create scoring opportunities
  • Use the receive zone to automatically position for returns
